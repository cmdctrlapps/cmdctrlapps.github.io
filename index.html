Building the Perfect Haptic Feedback

When we set out to build Spend, we didn't just want it to look good—we wanted it to feel right. Every time you log a transaction, there is a subtle haptic "click" that confirms your entry.

This mimics the tactile satisfaction of a physical ledger. In an era of digital float, making software feel physical is how we bridge the gap between intent and action.

Why we won't connect to your bank

The trend in fintech is automation. "Set it and forget it." We believe that is exactly the problem. When you forget about your spending, you lose agency over your capital.

Spend is built on the philosophy of Active Awareness. By manually entering your data, you acknowledge the trade-off of every purchase.
